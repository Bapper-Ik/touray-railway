The Sheikh Jassong Touray Foundation was based on the maining and promooting the legacy of late Sheikh Pa Jassong Touray. In our efforts to promote and maintain the legacy of our Grand Scholar and marabou the late Sheikh Pa Jassong Touray, We are embarking on a fund raising request from Donor Agencies, International Partners, NGO's and Charitable Organisations, to maintain his work. He led a Humble and Noble life. Most of His work was concentrated in the areas of Education, Health, Religion, Infrastructural Development, and work for Humanity and Agriculture.
