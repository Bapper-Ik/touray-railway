This compound is referred to as Muderr-derr which was a personal/private compound for Sheikh Jassong. Buiba Dallo, ARdul MAWAA - The Great Lake

This Great Lake is discovered by Sheikh Pa Jassong Touray, Every day people visit this lake to pray and take birth with its water. Farmers in the surrounding villages also take their cattle to drink at the place. Every annual gathering thousand gathered here to pray and take birth with water. We want to make a 2km road from the village to the site and plant some trees and erect seats around the place.

One of the reasons for his stay in Buiba is because of the lake called ‘ArdUl Mawaa’. He was informed about ‘Ardul Mawaa’ in his dream. It is 2 kilo metres on the south of Buiba. Jassong was looking for this lake for many years. When he has the permission from God, he gave out Sadaka (charity) of 7 types of food and made so many prayers before declaring the place as a secrete place. After the prayers and eating at the lake, he stood and asked one of his scholars to light a candle and place it on top of a cut-tree inside the lake. They came back to informed him and he then praised God and he commend; ‘whoever visits that lake after the completion of the candle their prayers will be answered by God’.

According to him, every 15th day of the month before Ramadan the Holy prophet (S. A. W.) and his Caliphs comes to ‘Ardul Mawaa’ Sins that are forgiven by God during the year are burn that day. During Jassong’s life time and even now, whoever embraced Islam is taken there for a formal visit.
